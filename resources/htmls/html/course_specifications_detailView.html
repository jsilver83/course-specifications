B. OBJECTIVES

List the main objectives of this course:

  1. Describe the various classes of organic compounds; and how to synthesize and analyze them.
  2. Identify and explain the structures of organic compounds from the corresponding infrared and nuclear magnetic resonance spectra.
  3. Describe the reaction mechanisms for reactions from the various classes of organic compounds
  4. Train the students on how to conduct organic compounds lab experiments safely, and how to analyze the experimental results.
